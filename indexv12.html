Our Story

A Century of
Exceptional Hospitality

Since 1924, The Grand Pavilion has stood as the pinnacle of refined hospitality. Our heritage suites and meticulously restored interiors are a testament to a bygone era — yet every detail has been elevated for the modern traveller.


Set amidst lush gardens and ocean-facing terraces, we offer not merely accommodation, but a sanctuary for those who seek beauty in everything.
